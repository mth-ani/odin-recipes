Tuna Artichoke Salad

Delicious and healthy salad!

Ingredients

  • 2 cans tuna, drained
  • 1 cup artichoke hearts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine tuna and artichoke hearts.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise and lemon juice.
  3. Pour the dressing over the tuna and artichoke mixture.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving.
