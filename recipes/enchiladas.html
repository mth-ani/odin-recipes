Enchiladas

Delicious Mexican dish!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Spread enchilada sauce in a baking dish.
  3. Fill each tortilla with chicken and cheese.
  4. Place filled tortillas in the baking dish.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes or until heated through.
